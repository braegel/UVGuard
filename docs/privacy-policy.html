Privacy Policy – UVGuard

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Overview

UVGuard is a UV index monitoring app that helps you stay safe from harmful UV radiation. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

2. Data We Collect

UVGuard only accesses the following data:

  • Approximate location (Coarse Location) – Used to retrieve the current UV index for your area. Only approximate location is used, not precise GPS coordinates.

The app does not collect any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, or other personal information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage statistics or analytics data
  • Contacts, photos, files, or any other content

3. How We Use Your Data

Your approximate location is used solely to query the current UV index and daily maximum UV value for your area from external weather services. Your location is not stored, not shared with third parties, and not used for any other purpose.

4. Third-Party Services

To retrieve UV index data, the app uses the following third-party APIs:

  • Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) – Open-source weather API. Only geographic coordinates (latitude, longitude) are transmitted. Privacy policy: open-meteo.com/en/terms
  • CurrentUVIndex – UV index API. Only geographic coordinates are transmitted.

All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. No user IDs, device information, or other data is sent to these services.

5. Data Storage

UVGuard does not store any data on external servers. All settings (such as the UV threshold for notifications) are stored locally on your device only.

6. Permissions

The app uses the following Android permissions:

  • Approximate Location (ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) – To retrieve the UV index for your area
  • Background Location (ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION) – To alert you about high UV radiation even when the app is in the background
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) – To warn you when the UV index exceeds your set threshold
  • Internet (INTERNET) – To fetch UV data from weather services
  • Foreground Service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE) – To periodically update UV data

7. Your Rights

Since UVGuard does not store or transmit any personal data, there is no stored data to access, correct, or delete. You can revoke the location permission at any time in your Android system settings. The app will continue to work but without UV data updates.

8. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated when new features are added. The current version is always available at this URL.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
